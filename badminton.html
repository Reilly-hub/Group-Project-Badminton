What is Badminton?

Badminton is a sport played using racquets and a net on a square court quite similarly to tennis, however instead of a ball participants will strike and keep airborne a small, feathered object called a shuttlecock or birdie. Also, unlike tennis, the shuttlecock in play should never touch the ground, or else that rally is over.

Matches are most commonly played one on one known as singles, or two on two called doubles. In competitive play the opposing teams are the same gender, or one man and woman each in the case of mixed doubles matches.

Due to its relatively low weight, players can accelerate the shuttlecock much faster than other racquet sports like tennis or squash, but thanks to its unique design, the shuttlecock will also decelerate much faster as it travels through the air.
